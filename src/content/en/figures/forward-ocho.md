---
title: Forward ocho
summary: A pivoting figure-eight in which the follower steps forward across the leader.
---

The follower dissociates toward the new direction, pivots on the standing leg,
and steps **forward** across the leader, tracing one half of a figure-eight.

Switch the **leader view** to see the two voicings: in *acompañando* the leader
walks with her; in *contrapeso* he stays closer to his own axis and changes
weight first — same follower line, different lead.
